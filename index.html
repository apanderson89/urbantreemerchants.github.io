The Benefits of Urban Harvesting

Urban harvesting offers numerous environmental benefits and plays a vital role in sustainable forestry practices. By salvaging trees that would otherwise be discarded as waste, we contribute to a greener future and a healthier urban ecosystem. Here are some of the key benefits of urban harvesting:

  • Reduced Waste: Instead of sending fallen or removed trees to landfills, we give them a second life by transforming them into valuable wood products.
  • Preserving Natural Resources: Urban harvesting reduces the demand for virgin lumber, helping to preserve natural forests and promote biodiversity.
  • Carbon Sequestration: Wood products from urban trees continue to store carbon, offsetting greenhouse gas emissions even after the trees are cut down.
  • Local Economy Support: By working with local Tree Care Companies, we support the local economy and strengthen the community.
  • Unique and Sustainable Products: Urban wood offers distinct characteristics, and by using it for furniture and decor, we create sustainable, one-of-a-kind pieces.

At Urban Tree Merchants, we are committed to making the most of our urban resources and promoting responsible wood use. Join us in the effort to create a more sustainable future.

What is Urban Wood?

Urban Wood, Urban Harvested, and Urban Timber refers to wood salvaged from tree removal operations when trees are cut down due to death, disease, development, or other causes. Traditionally, wood from these kinds of practices was treated as waste and, at best, used for mulch or firewood. We strive to give this valuable resource a second life in your home or office by converting the trees removed in the DFW Metroplex to lumber, furniture, decor, and other wood products. Learn more about the Urban Wood Movement at https://urbanwoodnetwork.org/.
